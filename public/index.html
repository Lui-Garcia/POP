Projects


Five-Year Marketing Plan

This is a marketing assignment conducted for a fictitious moving box company in which I had to develop a strategic five year marketing plan. The marketing plan includes various sections that take a detailed account of elements that are vital to the functioning of a business and are backed by heavy research. Different highlights of this project include strategic analysis, market-product focus, financial data and projections.

Strategic Bussiness Plan

This is a group assignment done in my international business class for the purpose of developing a strategic business plan for a real company to open operations to an overseas market. I worked deliberately and cohesively with fellow team members to plan, develop and execute the assignment in a timely and efficient manner. This project covers a wide set of subjects dealing with mission and vision, sustainability, market screen, competition, modes of entry, strategy and stakeholder analysis.

Cost Benefit Analysis

This is an assignment completed in my Bussiness Information Technology class where I had to create a workbook in Excel. This workbook is a cost benefit analysis conducted for a fictitious bike company where I analyze the feasibility of replacing the company’s current operating system software with a different one.

Informal Proposal

This group project was conducted in my Business Communication class. The task was to pick a real company to represent and persuade the Dallas Chamber of Commerce to invest in our campaign to give back to the community. Although the assignment was a simulation, my team and I presented this proposal to an audience of 30 and three judges. We worked as a team to produce a thoroughly researched and well organized campaign. The proposal includes various analyses, budget breakdown, staffing needs and overall operation management.
